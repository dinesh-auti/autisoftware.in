About

Built for the work that actually matters.

Auti is a boutique consultancy. We're small on purpose, selective by design, and senior across the board.

The founder

Dinesh Auti

Founder, Auti

I started Auti after years of building platform infrastructure and reliability systems for product companies across fintech, e-commerce, and health tech. The patterns I kept seeing — systems that were expensive to run, brittle to change, and owned by no one — had almost nothing to do with the technology and everything to do with who was making the decisions.

Auti exists because good engineering requires judgment, not just execution. The boutique model gives me the freedom to do the work right: small focused teams, clear accountability, and outcomes that stick after the engagement ends.

I'm based out of Mumbai and work with companies globally. Every engagement has me directly involved — not managing from a distance.

How we work

The team is built around the problem.

Discovery

We start by understanding the real problem.

Most engagements begin with a brief scoping call. We want to understand what you're trying to accomplish, what's blocking you, and whether we're the right fit. If we're not, we'll say so.

Assembly

We assemble the right team from a trusted network.

Depending on the engagement, I may bring in senior product managers, backend engineers, ML engineers, or platform specialists from a network I've built and trust. Everyone who works on your problem has done this type of work before.

Execution

We embed, build, and ship.

We work alongside your team — not as a black box. The goal is always to leave your team in a stronger position than when we arrived: better systems, clearer ownership, and the knowledge to maintain it.

Handoff

We hand off, we don't disappear.

Every engagement ends with documentation, runbooks, and knowledge transfer. We consider an engagement successful only when your team can fully own what we built together.

What we're not

We're deliberate about what we take on.

Not staff augmentation. We don't place engineers at clients. We own the outcome of an engagement, not just the hours.

Not a full-service agency. We work on focused, high-stakes engineering and product problems. We don't do brand work, SEO, or digital marketing.

Not volume-focused. We take on 2–3 engagements at a time. That's a deliberate constraint that lets us do the work well.

Ready to work together?

Tell us about your problem. If we're a good fit, we'll respond within 48 hours.
