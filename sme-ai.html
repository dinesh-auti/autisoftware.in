The problem

Most businesses are curious about AI. Few know where to start.

AI tools are powerful, but most businesses do not need another tool subscription or a generic AI strategy deck. They need someone who can understand their current process, identify where AI can create practical leverage, and ship something their team can actually use.

Common problems we see

  • Product and service information scattered across PDFs, WhatsApp, Excel, and old websites
  • Slow proposal or quotation creation
  • Inconsistent enquiry replies
  • Weak catalogs, landing pages, or company profiles
  • Repetitive admin work handled manually
  • Internal data trapped in spreadsheets
  • No simple owner dashboard
  • No structured follow-up or handover process
