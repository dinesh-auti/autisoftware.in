The starting point

Front desk under quiet pressure.

A small clinic does not have a queue management problem. It has a communication problem. The same questions arrive every day on different channels, and good intentions get lost between the doctor's chamber, the desk phone, and a personal WhatsApp.

What the day-to-day looks like

  • WhatsApp enquiries through the day, replied to when there is a gap
  • Missed calls during chair-side procedures
  • Repeated questions about timings, fees, and services
  • Manual appointment reminders, when there is time
  • Weak follow-up with patients who postponed
  • No owner visibility into enquiry volume or patterns

Asset 02

Sample FAQ response bank.

Five questions cover most front-desk traffic. Replies are short, factual, and identical every time.

Sample — Gangapur Dental Care FAQ replies

What are your timings?

We are open Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM to 7:30 PM. Sundays by appointment for follow-ups only.

How do I book an appointment?

Reply on this WhatsApp number with your preferred day and time, your main concern, and your full name. We confirm a slot within working hours.

What happens at the first visit?

A consultation, a brief examination, and — if needed — an X-ray. The doctor explains the findings and your options before anything is done.

How long does a consultation take?

Plan for around 30 to 40 minutes for a first visit. Follow-up consultations are usually shorter.

What should I bring?

Any past dental records or X-rays you have, a list of current medication, and a valid ID. If a child is the patient, please come with a parent or guardian.

Scope

Where AI helps without touching clinical records.

AI is used here only to support admin and communication at the front desk. It does not read, store, or generate clinical notes, prescriptions, diagnoses, or any sensitive medical information.

What it actually does

  • Classify each incoming enquiry by type — booking, reschedule, FAQ, or other
  • Draft replies from approved templates, ready for the front desk to review and send
  • Flag overdue follow-ups so warm enquiries are not forgotten
  • Summarize non-clinical front-desk activity at the end of each day
  • Prepare a short weekly owner report — enquiry volume, conversion, follow-ups due
  • Suggest FAQ improvements based on questions that come up repeatedly
