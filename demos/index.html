Reading guide

How to read these demos.

Each demo shows the same pattern: start with messy business material, identify one focused workflow, then create usable assets that a team can use immediately. In a real sprint, the outputs are adapted to the client's business, team, existing material, and tools.

A few things to keep in mind

  • These are fictional examples
  • They are not case studies or client results
  • The goal is to show the shape of possible outputs
  • Real engagements are scoped around one clear outcome
  • We avoid open-ended AI consulting and focus on execution
